# Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

Dhikr Discipline ("the App") is designed to be private by default.

## Summary

- No account is required.
- No ads are served.
- No analytics SDK is used.
- No tracking across apps or websites.
- Your progress data stays on your device.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers.

The App stores usage data locally on your device to make features work, including:

- Dhikr counts
- Daily completion and streak progress
- Ramadan dua completion state
- User preferences (language mode, font size, haptics, visibility toggles)

This local data is stored in browser/device storage (such as local storage and IndexedDB) inside the app container.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Tracking

We do not use tracking technologies for advertising or cross-app profiling.

## Internet Access

The app package may include standard internet permission required by the platform webview, but app progress data is not uploaded to our servers.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it.

You can delete your data by:

- Uninstalling the app, or
- Clearing the app's local storage/device data in system settings.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in future versions. If changed, we will update the effective date above.
